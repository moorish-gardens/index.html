Your place in the Atlas Mountains!

Moorish House is a detached holiday home, situated in Aghmat in the Marrakech-Tensift-El Haouz Region on the main road to Ourika Valley.

Our Facilities:
Breakfast Included Secure car park Hammam access Wheelchair access Free Wi-Fi

The house consist of twin bedroom, master bedroom, seating area, dining room with open plan kitchen, living room and a spacious family bathroom Towels and bed linen are available. Marrakech is 18.6 miles from Moorish House.

activities

Here at Moorish House, we believe in keeping fit and exploring what nature around us can offer us.

We have put up a set of activities for our clients that bring us together and so we can learn together about our locality and the surrounding areas.

Our Organized Tours ( i )

Local History Tour Landscape &Wildlife Tour Green Fingers Tour These tours offer you the best times with Moorish House.

Notes&Information

Water and tea with biscuits will be provided during these tours free of charge.To make a booking just ring our house Caretaker and we will make arrangements.

Safety Notice:

Please make sure you keep yourself watered and shaded at all times as the temperature during the day can reach high levels.

These tours are not recommended for parents with infants. Thank you for you cooperation.

Local History Tour

A local tour in the village for a day to get to know and discover the small hidden corners in the area, including a visit to Al-Mu'tamid ibn Abbad tomb. Open Market area and one of the oldest Hammams which is a historical site.

Fee per person: €25

Landscape&Wildlife Tour

This tour will consists mostly of visiting interesting areas within and nearby. A visit to the river during wet season and a walk through different douars (small inhibited pocket areas) A short walk towards Ourika Valley ,where you will get to enjoy the landscape including snow tipped mountains of the High Atlas!

Fee per person: €25

Green Fingers Tour

This tour will consists mostly of visiting local and nearby flower nurseries, giving you an introduction to the native plants and wild life. Decorative flowers ,shrubs and trees. During these visits you will get to meet local people and find out about their life style.

Fee per person: €20
